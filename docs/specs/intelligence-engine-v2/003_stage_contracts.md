# SPEC 003 — Stage Contracts

Define each pipeline stage like an API contract.

---

## signals

**Purpose:** Extract structured factual observations from dossier evidence.

No interpretation beyond local meaning.

---

## gtm_analysis

**Purpose:** Convert signals into commercial state descriptions.

**Outputs:**

- sales motion
- buyer structure
- distribution architecture
- founder dependency
- service dependency
- pricing/delivery tensions

---

## tensions

**Purpose:** Identify contradictions inside the GTM system.

**Examples:**

- enterprise narrative vs founder-led distribution
- product-led claim vs service-heavy onboarding
- developer usage vs executive pricing story

---

## patterns

**Purpose:** Cluster tensions into recurring failure archetypes.

---

## diagnosis

**Purpose:** Select one primary commercial diagnosis.

Exactly one.

---

## mechanisms

**Purpose:** Produce 2–3 plausible explanations for why the diagnosis exists.

---

## intervention_opportunities

**Purpose:** Define the most plausible agency-relevant fix.

---

## report

**Purpose:** Render a founder-facing document that is sharp, short, and credible.
