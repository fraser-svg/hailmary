# SPEC 006 — Evaluation and Quality Gates

Your current evals are too close to "did the text roughly say the thing."

Now you need stage-level quality gates.

---

## Evaluate

- does `gtm_analysis` correctly classify motion?
- does `diagnosis` choose the right archetype?
- are mechanisms plausible and grounded?
- does intervention fit agency-deliverable work?
- would the final report trigger curiosity?

---

## New Eval Dimensions

- specificity
- commercial relevance
- traceability
- founder resonance
- agency fit

---

## Hard Fail Conditions

- generic language
- unsupported diagnosis
- more than one primary diagnosis
- mechanism with no evidence
- intervention not implied by the diagnosis
- report reads like advice theatre
